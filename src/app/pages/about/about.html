ABOUT

Kombat Akademy was originally created by Raptor. In november 2024, Kombat Akademy was abandoned and open sourced

I'm Yimbo (@yimbofgc on Twitter). I picked up the abandoned project in 2026 and rebuilt it from the ground up, porting the entire WordPress/PHP site to Angular as a modern single-page application with updated data. All credit for the original content, data, and design goes to Raptor my work is just a preservation and modernization effort.

This version is open source on GitHub. If you'd like to contribute or report issues, feel free to reach out on Twitter.
