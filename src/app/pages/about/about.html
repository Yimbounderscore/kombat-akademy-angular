ABOUT

Kombat Akademy was originally created by Raptor — a fighting game enthusiast, former tournament player, and software developer. The site served as a comprehensive resource for Mortal Kombat 1, providing frame data, combos, guides, and more. After the Definitive Edition released, the original project was unfortunately abandoned.

I'm Yimbo (@yimbofgc on Twitter). I picked up the abandoned project in 2026 and rebuilt it from the ground up, porting the entire WordPress/PHP site to Angular as a modern single-page application. All credit for the original content, data, and design goes to Raptor — my work is just a preservation and modernization effort.

This version is open source on GitHub. If you'd like to contribute or report issues, feel free to reach out on Twitter.
